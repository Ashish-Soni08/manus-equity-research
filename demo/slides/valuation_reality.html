Valuation Reality Check: Trading at 3.8x Peer Median

Despite exceptional execution, Palantir's valuation is extreme by any measure. At 68.4x EV/Revenue, it trades at 3.8x the peer median of 16.8x.
Even the most expensive peers (CrowdStrike at 22.7x) trade at less than one-third of Palantir's multiple.
The Bottom Line: The market is pricing in a level of sustained growth and dominance that has historically been achieved by only a handful of companies in software history.
