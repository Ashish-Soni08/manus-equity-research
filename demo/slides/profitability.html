Profitability Inflection: Margins Expanding Across All Metrics

Palantir has undergone a dramatic profitability transformation, proving the operating leverage inherent in its business model.

Gross margins expanded to 82.4%, GAAP operating margin reached 31.6%, and adjusted operating margin hit 50.4%.

Free cash flow margin of 50.7% is exceptional for a software company at this growth stage.

Critically, stock-based compensation has declined from 50% of revenue in 2021 to just 15% in 2025, meaning GAAP profitability is increasingly real and sustainable.
